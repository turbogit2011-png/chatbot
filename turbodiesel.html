Technology

Equipment and procedures you won't find in an average workshop — that's why our parts simply work.

VSR balancing

VSR core balancing

Assembled cores are balanced at near-operating speed, eliminating the micro-vibrations that destroy bearings. The result: quiet running and OEM-level durability.

  • Dynamic full-core balancing
  • Real-time vibration measurement
  • Smooth, silent operation

Actuator calibration

Electronic actuators and VNT geometry programmed and calibrated against the control signal.

Injector test bench

Common-rail injectors tested on calibrated benches — spray pattern, back-leak and flow checked to spec.

Ultrasonic cleaning

Removes carbon and oil deposits from every channel without abrasion — production-level cleanliness.

Precision metrology

Micrometers and dial gauges — axial and radial clearances measured to 0.01 mm.
